ABOUT US

The bar is an ultimate oasis for your early evening and late-night gatherings, established in 2009. A metropolis themed decor rich in warm lighting and an exceptional hookah experience for both the hookah novice and the advanced social expert. The only LA-based hookah lounge to offer outdoor seating and a second-floor loft space!

Whether you're looking to relax on a weeknight or celebrate your next big event with the hottest DJ's in the Los Angeles Fridays and Saturdays, you've reached the right place! You must be 18 or older to enter. Weekend dress code is enforced.
